Mission Statement

So I’m just starting out with coding and my mission is to learn as much as I can, get better at it and eventually make some cool stuff that could help people. I’m studying at NSCC Institute of Technology, working on HTML and web development right now. I still have a lot to learn but I’m super excited about it. When I’m not coding I like to read, play video games and listen to music, those things help me chill and keep my mind creative. I want to keep getting better at coding and growing as a person, and maybe one day I can make something that actually makes a difference. For now I’m just gonna keep learning and doing my thing and see where it takes me.

My journey at NSCC Institute of Technology has been a wild ride so far. When I first started, I had no idea what I was getting into, but I knew I wanted to learn about technology. I remember feeling a little overwhelmed at first, especially with HTML and coding. The first few weeks were tough, and I wasn’t sure if I could keep up. But over time, I started to get the hang of it. I realized that coding isn’t just about writing lines of text—it’s about solving problems and making ideas come to life on a screen. Every time I figured something out or finished a project, I felt a huge sense of accomplishment. Even when things didn’t go as planned, I learned that failure is just part of the process, and it’s okay to ask for help when you need it. Outside of class, I’ve been experimenting with my own projects, making small websites, and just trying to put everything I’ve learned into practice. I also joined a few tech clubs at school, where I’ve met some really cool people who share the same interests. As I go through my first year, I’m starting to see how much there is to learn, and it’s exciting. There’s still a lot I don’t know, but every day I feel like I’m getting closer to where I want to be. I’m looking forward to exploring new technologies, building bigger projects, and someday being able to work on something that could really make a difference. Overall, NSCC has been an awesome place to start my tech journey, and I can’t wait to see where it takes me.
